# Stay Safe Online – My Personal Safety Checklist

## Protecting Your Identity

- [ ] I never share my Aadhar number, PAN card or bank details 
      with anyone over a call or through a link sent on WhatsApp
- [ ] I always verify a website's URL carefully before entering 
      any personal information — fake sites look almost identical 
      to real ones
- [ ] I never click on links sent in SMS or emails claiming to 
      be from my bank or government — I always go directly to 
      the official website instead
- [ ] I keep physical copies of my important documents safe and 
      never share photos of them casually on WhatsApp groups
- [ ] I regularly check my bank statements to spot any 
      transactions I did not make

## Account and Device Safety

- [ ] I use different strong passwords for different accounts 
      and never reuse the same one
- [ ] I have turned on two-step verification on my Gmail and 
      banking apps
- [ ] I lock my phone with a PIN or fingerprint so nobody can 
      access my apps if I lose it
- [ ] I keep my phone and apps updated because old versions 
      have security holes that hackers exploit

## UPI and Financial Safety

- [ ] I know that a UPI collect request takes money FROM me — 
      it is never a way to receive money
- [ ] I will never share my UPI PIN or OTP with anyone on a 
      call even if they claim to be from my bank or NPCI
- [ ] I have set a daily transaction limit on my UPI app to 
      reduce damage in case something goes wrong

## Social Media Safety

- [ ] I don't post photos of my ID cards, documents or tickets 
      on social media even as a joke
- [ ] I keep my Instagram and Snapchat accounts on private mode
- [ ] I don't accept requests from people I don't actually know

## If Something Goes Wrong

Report it immediately — the faster you act the better chance 
you have of stopping further damage

- Website: cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline: 1930 (free, available 24x7)
- Also call your bank's fraud helpline immediately
- Visit your nearest police station and file an FIR
- Save all screenshots, messages and transaction IDs as proof
